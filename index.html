RELIEF CARE

OBJECTIVE

A website which provides information about the health care assistance, food stamps, jobs and services related information, disaster relief, educational assistance and many more.

WHATS INNOVATIVE ?

Why searching for different services mentioned above in different websites when you can get all these in one website. Apart from providing information and services we are also planning to put a section of donations which includes - Money donation, Food donation and Clothes donation.

TYPES OF SERVICES

Health Care

Our objective is to provide health realted information to the common people in this pandemic situation and also provide assistance to the people who are in need of help by providing them free medicines, treatments and other neccessary materials.

Educational Assistance

Our objective is to provide educational assistance to our young aspirants preparing for any entrance exams and also help the poverty stricken, deprived students by giving them free books and free tutions.

Job Opportunities

Our objective is to provide you with latest job openings online including IT, Sales, Banking, Fresher, Walk-ins, Part time, Government jobs,etc. on our website.

Food Stamp

Our objective is to provide food benefits to poverty stricken, deprived people and also provide them education on food preparation and nutrition.

Disaster Relief

Our objective is to provide you with useful information related to disaster response and emergency response to provide immediate assistance to maintain life, improve health and support the morale of the affected people. The assistance includes providing aid, transport, temporary shelter, food, and many more.

Clothing

We as a non-profit organization aim to give services to poverty stricken, deprived people by giving them free clothes to make them feel comfortable during the cruel summer and winter season. We also appreciate your donations and help us to collect as many clothes as possible to keep the deprived ones comfortable in harsh summers and winters.

Donations

We as a non-profit organization are looking forward to partner with people who wants to contribute something to the society in form of any kind of donations starting from money, food, clothes to second hand books and many more. Any kinds of help and donations from your side is really appreciated. To know more about this please contact the owner in the contact section.

Helpline

For any kinds of help related to the mentioned services in the website feel free to contact the owner mentioned in the contact section. And also for more guidlines we will be providing you with goverment helpline numbers as well.
